Scientific Methodology

Aleatoric vs Epistemic

Conventional PPS models focus on aleatoric uncertainty (probability on a specific path). FRAM bridges the epistemic void—the risk of the unanalyzed spaces within a facility.

Normalization

To avoid double-counting, all inputs are scaled to [0,1]. This ensures model stability across different infrastructure sizes.

Fractal Algorithms

Box-Counting: Extracts Df (complexity). Measures how barriers fill the space.
Gliding-Box: Extracts L (Lacunarity). Measures the texture of sensor blind spots.

Weighting

Weights (w1, w2) allow expert calibration (e.g. via AHP) to prioritize either structural barriers or sensor coverage gaps.
