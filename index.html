2. Epistemic Uncertainty (FRAM Modifiers)
Epistemic Uncertainty (γ) 0.80
Proportion of the facility NOT analyzed. 0 = absolute certainty, 1 = extreme spatial blindness.
Fractal Dimension (Df) 1.60
Complexity of barriers. 1.0 = Flat open space. 2.0 = Highly complex labyrinth.
Normalized Lacunarity (L_norm) 0.50
Heterogeneity of sensor coverage. 0.0 = Perfect coverage. 1.0 = Massive clustered blind spots.
Complexity Weight (w1) 0.50
Adjusts the asymmetrical advantage of barriers vs. blind spots. (w2 is auto-calculated).
